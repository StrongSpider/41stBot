Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the 41st Event Portal, the associated Discord bot, related APIs, and connected features operated through 41st.spiider.dev and the 41st Discord server (collectively, the "Services") collect, use, store, and share information. By using the Services, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the Services, we may collect or store the following categories of information:

  • Discord account information such as user ID, username, avatar, roles, nicknames, and server membership details
  • Roblox account information such as Roblox user ID, username, verification or linking details, and related public profile data returned by Roblox
  • Portal session information used to keep you signed in and determine your access level
  • Event and quota data such as attendance, hosts, supervisors, event types, event points, edits, timestamps, and related audit history
  • Administrative or moderation data submitted through commands, dashboards, or API actions
  • Technical data such as request metadata, temporary cache entries, and error or system logs needed to operate and secure the Services
  • Limited browser storage data, such as the local high score saved by the website dino game feature

3. How We Collect Information

We collect information in several ways:

  • Directly from you when you use commands, log in through Discord, link a Roblox account, submit event information, or otherwise interact with the Services
  • Automatically from Discord or Roblox when authentication, verification, account linking, role checks, or API lookups are performed
  • Automatically through the operation of the Services, including session handling, server logs, and temporary caching
  • Through limited browser-side storage used by specific site features

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The portal uses session technology to keep authenticated users signed in. This may include a session cookie such as connect.sid. These session values are used for authentication, access control, and service security.

Certain front-end features may also store limited information in browser storage. At the time of this policy, the website dino game stores a local high score in your browser using localStorage. We do not use these features for advertising.

5. How We Use Information

We use collected information to operate, maintain, and improve the Services, including to:

  • Authenticate users and manage portal sessions
  • Verify or link Discord and Roblox accounts
  • Check permissions, roles, and access levels
  • Log, display, edit, and audit event and quota information
  • Maintain moderation, administrative, and operational records
  • Resolve bugs, secure the Services, prevent misuse, and investigate suspicious activity
  • Communicate policy or service updates when appropriate

6. Legal Bases and Permissions

Where applicable law requires a legal basis for processing personal information, we may rely on one or more of the following: your consent, performance of a requested service, compliance with legal obligations, and our legitimate interests in operating, securing, and administering the Services.

7. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information and we do not use collected data for advertising. We may share information only in limited circumstances, including:

  • With Discord and Roblox when needed for authentication, verification, public account lookups, role validation, or related integrations
  • With authorized administrators, officers, or moderators who need the information to operate the Services
  • When required to comply with law, legal process, or a valid governmental request
  • When necessary to protect the rights, safety, integrity, or security of the Services, the community, or other users

8. Data Retention

We keep information only for as long as it is reasonably needed for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including operating the Services, maintaining records, resolving disputes, enforcing rules, and complying with legal obligations.

Some data is temporary. For example, session information and internal cache entries may expire automatically. Other records, such as verification links, event history, audit logs, or administrative records, may be retained longer unless removed or no longer needed.

9. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to help protect information under our control. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, or deletion of certain personal information. You may also be able to object to or restrict certain processing.

You can also stop using the Services at any time, log out of the portal, or remove the bot from a server if you have authority to do so. Requests relating to stored data may be sent to spiider.dev@gmail.com. We may ask for information needed to verify the request before acting on it.

11. Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 outside the account systems and platforms they use to access the Services. If you believe a child has provided personal information inappropriately, please contact us.

12. Third-Party Services and Links

The Services may rely on or link to third-party services, including Discord and Roblox. Those third parties maintain their own privacy practices, and we are not responsible for the privacy policies or content of third-party services.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Services after an update becomes effective means you acknowledge the revised Privacy Policy.

14. Contact Us
